Our Story

The Name Behind the Mission

A name built on people

architecture + anthropos = human-centered design

Anthropos, the ancient Greek word for human, reminds us that at the heart of every great building, every innovative design, and every efficient workflow is the human experience. Architecture isn't just about structures, it's about creating spaces where people thrive.

We don't believe technology should replace the human touch, it should enhance it. That's why Lintelos exists: to create tools that empower architects to focus on what they do best, thinking critically, designing beautifully, and building meaningfully.

We build intelligent Revit add-ins and automation systems that eliminate the repetitive, inhuman parts of the job, not to chase efficiency for its own sake, but to make space for deeper work, creativity, and care.
